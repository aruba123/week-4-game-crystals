Crystal Collector

How to play the game


  1. You are given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. When you click on a crystal, a specific amount of points will be added to your total score.
  4. By matching your total score to the random number, wins the game!.
  5. The game is lost if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. At the beginning of each game, the values of each crystal changes .
